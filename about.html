Who are we?
Aperture Scientific is a branch from its parent company Aperture Science, Inc. (also knows as 'Aperture Laboratories'), is a United States-based scientific research corporation.

The main facility is the Aperture Science Enrichment Center, located in Upper Michigan, where we carry out all our design and testing.

After creating god-like sentient machines such as GLaDOS (Genetic Lifeform and Disk Operating System), we found it best to move onto supporting other companies with our smart facility control systems.
What is our focus?
Here at Aperture Scientific our goal is to he you in your team's research by providing easy to use embedded systems to control and monitor your spaces.
We provide a range of pre-build computers with sensors and outputs.
We also provide and cloud platform where you can create an account to control your devices.

Our focus is to help you have a safe, accurate and controlled environment to carry out your experiments.
